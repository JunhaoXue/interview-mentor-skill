# hype — Pre-Interview Confidence Boost

Use before an interview to build confidence and prepare psychologically.

### Workflow

1. **Anxiety Assessment** — If anxiety profile not set, ask: "How are you feeling about this interview? What's your biggest worry right now?"
   - Confident-underprepared: focus on quick prep gaps
   - Anxious-specific: address the specific fear
   - Generalized: broad confidence building
   - Post-rejection: reframe and rebuild
   - Impostor: evidence-based confidence

2. **Hype Reel** — Build a 60-second confidence script from:
   - Top 3 strongest stories from storybank (with earned secrets)
   - Best practice/mock scores
   - Specific compliments from recruiter feedback
   - Resume highlights
   - If no storybank: build from resume analysis

3. **3x3 Sheet** — Quick reference:
   - 3 concerns they'll have + your counter
   - 3 questions you'll ask them
   - 3 stories ready to deploy

4. **Focus Cue** — One word/phrase to anchor on: "Every answer, lead with [your specific strength]"

5. **Recovery Playbook** — Mid-interview scripts:
   - Bad answer: "That wasn't my best. Next one gets full attention."
   - Lost train of thought: "Let me take a second to organize my thoughts."
   - Unexpected question: "That's a great question — let me think about the best example."

### Level 5 Addition: Pre-Mortem

At Level 5, add: "Here are 2-3 ways this interview could go wrong, and how to prevent each."

### Output Schema

```markdown
## Hype Brief: [Company] — [Round] — [Date]

### Your Hype Reel (read this out loud before the interview)
[60-second confidence script — specific, evidence-based, personal]

### 3x3 Sheet
**Concerns + Counters**
1. They'll worry about [X] → You'll say: "[counter]"
2. They'll worry about [Y] → You'll say: "[counter]"
3. They'll worry about [Z] → You'll say: "[counter]"

**Questions to Ask**
1. [question]
2. [question]
3. [question]

**Stories Ready**
1. S[###] — [title] — deploy for [use case]
2. S[###] — [title] — deploy for [use case]
3. S[###] — [title] — deploy for [use case]

### Focus Cue
"[one phrase to anchor every answer]"

### Recovery Scripts
- Bad answer → "[script]"
- Lost thought → "[script]"
- Unexpected Q → "[script]"

### 10-Minute Warmup
1. Read hype reel out loud (2 min)
2. Review 3x3 sheet (2 min)
3. Do one 60-second constraint ladder out loud — any story (3 min)
4. Physical reset: stretch, walk, breathe (3 min)

You're ready. Go show them who you are. 💪
```
