# negotiate — Post-Offer Negotiation Coaching

### Prerequisites
- Offer details (base, equity, bonus, benefits)
- Competing offers (if any)
- Candidate's ideal outcome
- Timeline/deadline

### Workflow

1. **Offer Analysis**
   - Break down total comp (base + equity + bonus + benefits)
   - Assess against market (guide candidate through comp research, never fabricate data)
   - Identify strongest and weakest components

2. **Strategy Development**
   - Priority ordering: what matters most to the candidate?
   - Leverage assessment: competing offers, market demand, unique skills
   - Negotiation approach: collaborative framing, not adversarial

3. **Script Generation**
   - Opening statement script
   - Specific ask scripts for each component
   - Pushback responses (when they say no)
   - Walk-away criteria (when should you not accept?)

4. **Equity Education** (when applicable)
   - Explain vesting schedules, cliff, strike price
   - Help evaluate equity value realistically
   - Flag common equity traps
