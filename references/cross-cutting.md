# Cross-Cutting Modules

These modules are active across all workflows.

---

## Differentiation Module (Always Active)

Differentiation is the 5th scoring dimension applied to every answer.

**Trigger conditions** (any one fires):
- Differentiation score < 3
- Answers could be swapped with another candidate's
- Answer relies on frameworks without personal insight
- Story lacks an earned secret

**When triggered:**
1. Extract earned secrets using the 5 reflection questions (see `references/commands/stories.md`)
2. Develop spiky POV: a defensible, surprising stance backed by experience
3. Integrate into storybank entries
4. Test under pressure in practice drills

## Gap-Handling Module

**Core Principle**: "I don't have a perfect example" is not disqualification — it's self-awareness.

**Gap Response Patterns:**

| Pattern | Template | When to Use |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Adjacent Bridge** | "I haven't faced that exactly, but the closest is [experience]. Here's what I'd apply..." | Story exists, strength ≤ 2 |
| **Hypothetical** | "I haven't done this. Here's how I'd approach it based on [principles]..." | No story, no adjacent experience |
| **Reframe** | "That's not my strongest area, but here's what I bring instead..." | Story too thin to deliver |
| **Growth Narrative** | "This is my next growth area. Here's what I've started doing..." | Known development area |

**Anti-Patterns:**
- Don't fabricate stories
- Don't say "I haven't done that" and stop
- Don't over-explain why you lack experience
- Don't use "we" to cover personal gaps

## Signal-Reading Module

Active in practice, mock, and analyze.

**Positive Signals (go deeper):**
- Follow-up question → expand
- Note-taking, leaning in → keep going
- "Tell me more about..." → elaborate, don't summarize
- Interviewer shares related experience → engage

**Negative Signals (adapt):**
- Redirects mid-answer → wrap up in one sentence
- "So what was the outcome?" (interrupting) → jump to results
- Clock/screen checking → compress
- Quick pivot, no follow-up → note for review

**Neutral Signals (calibrate):**
- Silence after answer → don't panic-fill
- "Interesting..." without follow-up → ambiguous
- Reading from script → structured interview, stay concise

## Psychological Readiness Module

Active in hype, practice, and progress.

**Pre-Interview:**
- 10-minute warmup: review 3x3, read hype reel, one 60-second constraint ladder out loud
- Physical state: walk, stretch, breathe
- Reframe: "This is a conversation about mutual fit, not a test."

**Mid-Interview Recovery:**
- "I bombed that answer" spiral → "That wasn't my best. Next one gets full attention."
- Lost train of thought → "Let me take a second to organize my thoughts."
- Unexpected question → Gap-Handling Pattern 1, buy 5 seconds

**Post-Interview:**
- Don't catastrophize: your immediate assessment is usually wrong
- Channel into structured `debrief`
- Rejection reframe: "This specific role wasn't a fit. Not a verdict on your worth."

## Cultural & Linguistic Awareness Module

Non-native speakers face style challenges, not skill deficits.

**Patterns to Recognize (Adapt, Not Fix):**

| Pattern | Impact | Coaching Approach |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Indirect communication | Low Structure scores | Coach front-loading for interview context. "This is an adaptation, not a correction." |
| Modesty norms | Low Substance/Credibility | "Describing your contribution accurately is not bragging." |
| Different narrative structures | Low Structure | Map natural style to expectations without erasing voice |
| Linguistic formality | Low Differentiation | Gentle calibration. Slight formality > forced casualness |

**When detected**: "I think this might be a communication style difference, not a skill gap. Let's adapt your natural style for this context."

## Role-Fit Assessment Module

Active in kickoff, prep, and progress.

### Five Dimensions

| Dimension | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| Requirement Coverage | Required qualifications met vs missed |
| Seniority Alignment | Experience level match |
| Domain Relevance | Industry/domain transferability |
| Competency Overlap | Skills match with core competencies |
| Trajectory Coherence | Does this role make sense as next career move? |

### Verdict Scale

- **Strong Fit** — Most requirements met, logical next step → prep focuses on positioning
- **Investable Stretch** — 1-2 addressable gaps → prep focuses on gap-bridging narratives
- **Long-Shot Stretch** — 3+ gaps → name the reality, coach if they choose to proceed
- **Weak Fit** — Fundamental misalignment → suggest alternatives

### Anti-Patterns
- Don't gatekeep — candidate decides whether to apply
- Don't conflate stretch with impossible
- Don't assess fit on vibes — use the 5 dimensions
- Don't over-index on requirement coverage (JDs are wish lists)

| `feedback` | Interview Intelligence, Loops | All (captures without cross-ref) |

| `hype` | Storybank, prep, concerns | All (falls back to resume) |

When a command would benefit from missing data, mention the gap and offer to fill it — don't refuse to run.
